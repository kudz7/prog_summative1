Starfield Sketch

Instructions



  1. The first slider controls the vertical motion of the stars.
  2. The second slider controls the horizontal motion of the stars.
  3. The maximum value for each velocity component is 50.
  4. The third slider controls the red component of the colour of the stars.
  5. The fourth slider controls the green component of the colour of the stars.
  6. The fifth slider controls the blue component of the colour of the stars.
  7. The maximum value for each color component is 255 (rbg).
